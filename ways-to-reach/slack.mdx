<Info>
  Working in Slack? Message Mirathis directly in your workspace for quick assistance\!
</Info>

## Setup

1. Go to your [Mirathis dashboard](https://mirathis.app).
2. Navigate to Integrations \> Slack.
3. Click "Connect to Slack".
4. Authorize Mirathis to access your Slack workspace.
5. Choose which channels Mirathis can access.

## Usage

Once connected, you can:

- Direct message @Mirathis in any channel
- Mention @Mirathis in team conversations
- Use slash commands like `/mirathis schedule meeting`

Mirathis will respond directly in Slack, keeping your workflow seamless.
