## What is Mirathis?

Mirathis is your intelligent personal assistant that seamlessly integrates with your daily workflow. Whether you need help managing your schedule, handling communications, or staying organized, Mirathis works across all your favorite platforms to keep you productive and focused.

## Key Features

Mirathis transforms how you interact with technology by providing:

- **Unified Communication**: Handle phone calls, SMS, WhatsApp, email, and Slack from one intelligent interface
- **Smart Scheduling**: Automatic calendar management and meeting coordination
- **Task Management**: Intelligent reminders and to-do tracking
- **Information Retrieval**: Quick access to search results and research
- **Voice & Text**: Natural conversation through multiple channels

## Getting Started

Setting up Mirathis is simple and takes just a few minutes. Connect your communication channels, integrate your favorite tools, and start experiencing the power of having a personal assistant that never sleeps.
