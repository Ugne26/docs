## Automated Features

Through your inbox preferences in the [Mirathis web app](https://mirathis.app), you can enable Mirathis to deliver a daily summary of your email activity. This capability is exclusively available for Pro subscribers.

## Sample Commands

- "Look for new messages from [alex@company.com](mailto:alex@company.com) from today."
- "Compose a response to Maria's latest email confirming I'm available tomorrow at 10am."
- "Read the project update email aloud to me."
- "Send the proposal document to [director@company.com](mailto:director@company.com) with a request for feedback."
- "Share the meeting link with Tom for today's scheduled call."

## Setup (web)

1. Navigate to the inbox section in your [Mirathis dashboard](https://mirathis.app).
2. Select the configuration icon in the upper right corner.
3. Choose "Link Gmail" or "Link Outlook".
4. You'll be directed to authenticate with your chosen email service.

## Setup (iOS app)

1. Open the profile icon in the [Mirathis app](https://mirathis.app).
2. Navigate to "Integrations", then select "Inbox".
3. Choose "Link Gmail" or "Link Outlook".
4. You'll be directed to authenticate with your chosen email service.
