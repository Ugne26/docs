## Integrations

Mirathis syncs with your Slack accounts and workspaces.

<Card title="Slack" icon="slack" iconType="duotone" horizontal={false}>
  Connect up to 5 Slack workspaces with your Mirathis account.
</Card>

## Example Commands

- "Check for new messages in #general."
- "Reply to @random with 'I'm on it.'"
- "Send @Ethan a message with the Zoom link for the meeting today."
- "React to @Ethan's message in #random with a thumbs up."
- "Pin the message I just sent in #general."

## Setup (web)

1. Go to your [Mirathis dashboard](https://mirathis.app).
2. Click the integration settings button at the top of the side bar.
3. Click "Slack".
4. Click "Connect Workspace".
5. You will be redirected to Slack to sign in.

## Setup (iOS app)

1. Tap the account icon in the [Mirathis app](https://mirathis.app).
2. Under "Integrations", select "Slack".
3. Click "Connect Workspace".
4. You will be redirected to Slack to sign in.
