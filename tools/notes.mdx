## Example Commands

- [Forward an email to Mirathis](/interfaces/email) and say "Save the action items from this email to my notes."
- [Text an image to Mirathis](/interfaces/sms) and say "Add all the dates from this flyer to my notes under 'Travel Dates'."
- [Call Mirathis](/interfaces/phone) and say "Note down everything we talked about on this call and call it 'Brain Dump'."
- [Call Mirathis](/interfaces/phone) and say "Save all the ideas we discussed on this call as a note and add it to my 'Project X' note."

## Setup (web)

1. In your [Mirathis dashboard](https://mirathis.app), you can create, view, and manage your notes in the "Notes" app.

## Setup (iOS app)

- In the [iOS app](https://mirathis.app), tap the account icon and under "Tools", tap "Notes" to manage your notes.
