## Example Briefing Topics

- My schedule for today
- Sports and Finance news
- Important emails from my team
- My to-do list
- Weather updates

## Setup (web)

1. Go to the briefings card in your [Mirathis dashboard](https://mirathis.app).
2. Click the settings button in the top right.
3. Select a frequency and list of topics for your briefings.

## Setup (iOS app)

1. Tap the account icon in the [Mirathis app](https://mirathis.app).
2. Under "Tools", select "Briefings".
3. Select a frequency and list of topics for your briefings.
