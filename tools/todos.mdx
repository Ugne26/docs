## Background Tasks

In your To-dos Settings in the [Mirathis web app](https://mirathis.app), you can configure Mirathis to text you a list of your to-dos every morning. This feature is currently only available to Pro users.

## Example Commands

- "Add each of my starred emails from today to my engineering to-do list."
- "Add the meetings from my calendar today to my to-do list."
- "Mark all my work-related todos as complete."
- "Add 'Finish email campaign' to my marketing to-do list."
- "Add 'Eric' and 'Andrew' to my customer list."

## Setup (web)

- In your [Mirathis dashboard](https://mirathis.app), you can create, view, and manage your to-dos in the "To-dos" card.
- You can also import your to-dos from Todoist in the [Mirathis web app](https://mirathis.app) → Settings → To-dos.

## Setup (iOS app)

- In the [iOS app](https://mirathis.app), tap the account icon and under "Tools", tap "To-dos" to manage your to-dos.
